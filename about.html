About Me

Carter Quickel is a charismatic, probably not-so-handsome student who has one goal: change the world. His life dream is to make games with/for others.
He has a very cool YouTube channel that you should check out: RoyalCookiez!
He had many ideas that came to mind when he created his RPG Maker MZ game, but knew he had limits (mostly his spinal column).
He wants to have a minute to shout out his friends who have helped him along the way. There names aren't important because we have a low budget.

In his free time, Carter likes to draw, work on Unreal Engine 5 or Blender, or play video games.
Some of his favorites are Horizon: Forbidden West, Hollow Knight, The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, and Bioshock: Infinite.
He lives with his mom, dad, sister, and golden lab!
"Hope you enjoy my game!"
